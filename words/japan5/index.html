Kyoto (day 4)

“sitting is for the weak… but I’m weak” - Rain

that quote from my younger brother encapsulates the day as we took a bus home, it works better than any description i can write.

RECAP

We started at the Arashiyama bamboo forest. We were out early to catch a less crowded bamboo forest, but it was still difficult to take pictures without tourists in the background. If I were to ever revisit these famous Japanese destinations, I would definitely catch them at the break of dawn, as crowds of tourists like ourselves don't do them justice.

We didn't stay fresh for long as we took a steep hike up Mt. Arashiyama to Iwatayama, a snow monkey sanctuary. Lots of monkeys, lots of sweat.

Finished our tourist adventures for the day with the Golden Pavillon and Nishiki market, where there were tons of delicious eats. The market, not the gold clad building.
