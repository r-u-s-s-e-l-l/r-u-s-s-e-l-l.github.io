5, 6, 7, 8, typhoon!

In honour of the approaching typhoon today, here’s a typhoon of fast facts because I’ve been too tired to keep up with writing the past couple days (aha an excuse for procrastination):

  1. our last day in Kyoto was more or less uneventful (especially because I was very, very tired. ) We did some more sightseeing and revisited the Gion district.

  2. the next day was spent traveling to Hiroshima. (we weren’t able to get reserved seats so we had to stand for a large part of the ride) Visited one of the most profound and insightful exhibits I’ve seen in a while at the Hiroshima Peace Memorial Museum.

  3. Okonomiyaki + Gyoza <3

  4. Miyajima island was beautiful. However, highly recommend visiting Japan NOT in August as the weather is … grimy.

  5. Our host in Hiroshima was very lovely!! Sora was a beautiful guest house. Thank you Coco for a wonderful stay and even more thanks for offering to drive us to the train station at 5:30AM in the morning when all the taxis were booked!! There’s good people out there :)

  6. We’re now living in an apartment-hotel in Osaka. Osaka has a lot more grit, living up to the contrast that Japan is known for.
