faceless

You with your unadorned silence.

You’re empty amid the storm’s fullness.

The noise should stir.

It should sadden or anger, flesh out your being, yet stillness is found between your every sickening emotion.

Partnership.

Singularity?

Impartiality is your downfall. Your memory blank, symptom of the mundane.

You wait and wait until you lose control.

Or is this your place of sanity?

Perhaps I shall leave you be.
